About

Laugh Victoria is the central home for comedy in Victoria, British Columbia. Every stand-up show, open mic, and improv night happening in the city, listed in one place, updated every week.

Victoria's comedy scene has grown faster than most people realize. Since COVID the scene didn't slow down, it leveled up. Independent producers stepped up, new shows launched, and the city developed the kind of weekly comedy infrastructure that most cities twice its size don't have.

Laugh Victoria exists because that scene deserves a front door. A place where fans can find tonight's show, aspiring comics can find their first stage, established locals can stay connected, and anyone visiting from out of town can see immediately that something real is happening here.

Mission
To make Victoria's comedy scene impossible to miss.
Vision
Victoria is nationally recognized as one of the best comedy cities in Canada, and the world knows it.
Values
🎭
By the scene, for the scene.
Victoria belongs to the comics who get on stage, the producers who run the rooms, and the fans who keep showing up. We're just here to make sure everyone can find it.
🌊
A rising tide lifts all ships.
A packed room at one show is good for every show. A comic who blows up is good for every comic. There's no competition here, only a shared interest in building something worth being part of.
🚪
No gatekeeping.
If you're doing comedy in Victoria, you belong here. Open mic, headliner, first time on stage, tenth year on the road. Every level, every venue, every kind of funny. Even improv.
📍
If people can't find it, it doesn't exist.
The best show in the city means nothing if nobody knows it's happening. Every show deserves an audience and we're here to make sure nothing gets lost and Victoria's comedy shows get found.
